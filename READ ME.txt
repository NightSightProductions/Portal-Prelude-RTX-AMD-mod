 Instructions:
  1. Select your architecture
  2. Drag and drop contents of dxvk-patch into .trex folder in Portal Prelude RTX bin
  3. Drag and drop one of the rtx.conf presets into Portal Prelude RTX directory, make sure to replace existing rtx.conf

 Warning: While in ALT + X dev menu, do not go to the remix graphics menu, this will revert some changes and reduce performance

 If you need support, you can join our discord through the mod site

  How to know your architecture:
   RDNA 2: 6000 series, 600m series
   RDNA 3: 7000 series, 700m series

   *to fix dark areas, go to post processing in the dev menu and change eye adaptation from median to mean

 We would love feedback for other GPUs
